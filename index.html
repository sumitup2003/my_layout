Places I visited in Hyderabad

  • There are many places in Hyderabad to hangout. It includes many historical places of ancient time. I personally have visited many places of Hyderabad!

  • Let's start with Golconda, it is one of the oldest fort in India. It was built by Quli Qutub shah emperor, it was also called old Hyderabad.

  • Charminar is also a old architecture it was built many years ago. charminar is the Identity of Hyderabad. There are lot's of variety of markets or bazaars over there.

  • Birla Mandir i guess everyone knows this temple many people visit this holy place. It is always packed with full of devotees.

  • Ramoji Film City it is one of the biggest film city in India it is very popular. Many movies, series, serials are shot here.

  • Wonderla it is the place where people can relax and spend time enjoying the rides. There are many different types of rides which includes water rides and land rides.

  • Swarnagiri it is one of my favourite places. I felt very good after visiting This holy place. People from different parts come here to offer prayer to lord Venkateshwara Swamy.
